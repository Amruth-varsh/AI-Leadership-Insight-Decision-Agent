Q: How did Digital Media perform compared to last year?

A: Digital Media revenue grew from $15.864 billion in fiscal 2024 to $17.649 billion in fiscal 2025, representing an 11% year-over-year increase. This growth was driven by increases in revenue associated with Creative and Document Cloud subscription offerings. The segment's revenue also accounted for 74% of the company's total revenue in both fiscal 2024 and 2025. Digital Media Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) grew to $19.20 billion at the end of fiscal 2025, representing 11.5% year-over-year growth.
