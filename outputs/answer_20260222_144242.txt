Q: What is Adobe's FY2026 earnings per share guidance?

A: Answer: Adobe's FY2026 earnings per share guidance is $17.90 to $18.10 on a GAAP basis and $23.30 to $23.50 on a non-GAAP basis. The company expects to achieve this guidance based on its targets for total revenue, subscription revenue, and total Adobe ending ARR growth. The targets assume a non-GAAP operating margin of ~45.0%, a GAAP tax rate of ~20.5%, and a non-GAAP tax rate of ~18.0%.
